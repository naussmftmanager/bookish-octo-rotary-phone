Services

Angela Nauss is a trauma specialist who believes that everyone should have access to mental health care and appreciates working with clients to maintain recovery. Former clients have self-reported a reduction in nightmares, intrusive memories, and thoughts, challenging negative self-beliefs, and building resiliency skills. Clients also self-report success resolving traumatic childhood memories and challenging toxic relationships. She works with clients to achieve goals and become more productive.

Mission

Well being is important to a life well lived. I want you to have well being and the best opportunities. I welcome the opportunity to become a part of your mental health journey. Together we can build a plan for positive change through mental health therapy.
