Transcript

I am a trauma specialist who believes that everyone should have access to mental health care. My experience with co-morbid mental health disorders includes: trauma, depression, anxiety, panic disorder, and bipolar disorder. I have conducted therapy sessions in shelters, recovery centers, on the beach, in apartments, and in janitor closets but I’ve recently moved to telehealth sessions during the pandemic. Former clients have self-reported a reduction in nightmares, intrusive memories and thoughts. Clients also self-report success challenging negative self-beliefs and building resiliency skills.

Wholeness

I provide therapeutic solutions for people who can’t remember a time when they weren’t lonely. Symptoms feel like depression some days and anxiety on others. Someone who turns their isolation inwards, and asks themselves, “what’s wrong with me?” Maybe other people’s lives seem easier by comparison. I get it. I believe that our cultural obsession with work is what drives people to feel isolated. I don’t think anyone should have to feel like they don’t have time for their own life. I want to help clients re-integrate into society and rediscover the things they used to love.

I look forward to working with you!
